What's the story?

I am a seasoned educator with 21 years of rich experience in education with a B. Ed and an M.A. (Economics) degrees from a reputed university. I create dynamic and inclusive learning environments, fostering critical thinking and love for learning. Strong partnerships with parents and the community contribute to holistic student development. Passionate, accomplished, and dedicated to student success, I am committed to making a positive impact in education.

With strong critical thinking skills, I am able to consider the best interests of my students with set goals and standards. I also focus on parent’s expectations for learning and development of the child. I ensure to provide disciplined , learning as well as safe & nurturing environment.

As a teacher I work closely with my students to make sure they acquire not only the material at hand, but also the skills needed to continue learning own their own. I always provide a friendly and energetic atmosphere which allows my students to feel comfortable to ask questions and show their understanding of the content. In my free time, I enjoy spending time outside, doing craft work and painting. Over the years I acquired many tutoring tips and tricks that lead my students to rewarding “ Aha ” moment of understanding the material.
